Stewart Harding: The Man, The Myth, The Legend

Stewart Harding, aka Stukulele is a Norfolk=born, Cardiff-based Runner with a capital R. He has established himself as a key figure in local club circles and is an experienced run leader, having achieved several qualifications, including:

  • Leadership in Running Fitness
  • Fell, Hill, Trail and Off-Road Leader
  • Club Chairman (Former)
He is also involved in leadership at a regional level and actively involved in the organisation and management of local trail running events.
